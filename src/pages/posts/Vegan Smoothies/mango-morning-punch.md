**Ingredients:**

\- 1cm Fresh Ginger root

\- ½ Alfonso Mango (Mexican)

\- 1x Orange

\- 1x Ruby red grapefruit

\- ½ Lime juice (optional)

**Preparation (10 min):**

Peel & cut the ginger in thin slices, mix everything with the hand blender until smooth
